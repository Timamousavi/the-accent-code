Course Structure: The 8-Week Transformation

A clear, step-by-step roadmap to fluency and confidence.

Module 1 • Week 1
The Neuroscience of a New Accent

Learn the science behind accent modification, set a baseline with a diagnostic recording, and understand how your brain will form new neural pathways for American English.

Module 2 • Weeks 2-3
The Building Blocks - American English Phonetics

Master individual American English sounds by learning the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA) and practicing difficult sounds like the American 'R' and Flap 'T'.

Module 3 • Week 4
The Music of Speech - Prosody & Intonation

Go beyond individual sounds to understand the "music" of American English, including stress, rhythm, and intonation patterns that carry meaning.

Module 4 • Week 5
Connected Speech - The Secrets of Natural Flow

Discover the "shortcuts" of natural, flowing speech, such as linking words and reductions, to sound more fluent and less like you are "over-enunciating."

Module 5 • Weeks 6-7
Active Training & Integration

Put all the skills together with advanced practice techniques like scientific shadowing, where you analyze and mimic native speakers to refine your skills.

Module 6 • Week 8
Real-World Application & Lifelong Practice

Transition from structured practice to confident, real-world application. Re-record your diagnostic paragraph to see your incredible progress and create a lifelong practice plan.
